# Simpro Competitor Analysis

**Purpose:** Readable index for Simpro competitor positioning, battlecard access, and current SEO market implications.
**Use when:** Planning competitive content, comparison pages, alternatives angles, or competitor-aware proof strategy.
**Owns:** Competitor strategy summary, priority competitor matrix, battlecard index, and maintenance cadence.
**Does not own:** Full imported battlecards or detailed SEO market evidence; those live in reference files.
**Source boundary:** Point-in-time battlecard, DataForSEO, Ahrefs Free, Semrush, and dashboard evidence through 2026-05-22 unless refreshed.
**Refresh cadence:** Quarterly, or earlier when new battlecards, pricing, AI-feature claims, or major SERP shifts land.
**Reference detail:** SEO market evidence: [seo-market-signals.md](reference/competitors/seo-market-signals.md); battlecards: [battlecards directory](reference/competitors/battlecards/).

## How to Use This File

Use this file as the fast entrypoint for competitor-aware content decisions. Start here for the strategic readout, then open the linked reference files only when you need exact battlecard wording, source boundaries, or detailed SEO market evidence.

## Source Boundaries

- Full SEO market signals, source notes, dashboard limitations, Ahrefs/Semrush metrics, opportunity matrix, watching list, and maintenance guidance are preserved in [seo-market-signals.md](reference/competitors/seo-market-signals.md).
- Full battlecard wording is preserved one competitor per file in [battlecards](reference/competitors/battlecards/).
- Treat all competitor pricing, rankings, backlinks, AI Overview findings, and SERP slices as point-in-time evidence. Recheck before public claims.
- ServiceTitan is a confirmed named competitor with a full April 2026 battlecard. Use the [ServiceTitan battlecard](reference/competitors/battlecards/servicetitan.md) for full detail.
